Recognize the warning signs

Red flags on packaging

  • “Not for human consumption” / “Research chemical”
  • Small colorful vials or jars with gas station logos
  • Names like “Za Za,” “Tianaa,” “Pegasus,” “Neptune’s Fix” (tianeptine)
  • “7‑OH,” “Pseudo,” “Ultra,” “Blacked Out” labels
  • QR codes that link to grey‑market dosing instructions

Take action today

  • Report suspicious products to the FDA via MedWatch
  • Contact local health departments and law enforcement
  • Advocate for state bans on tianeptine and unlabeled synthetics
  • Share this guide with schools, parent groups, and community coalitions
