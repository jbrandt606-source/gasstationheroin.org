Tips for making an impact

Be specific Quote the misleading language exactly as it appears on the packaging or advertisement.
Include photos Take clear pictures of the product, label claims, and where it was sold.
Provide location Include the store name and address where you saw the product.
Stay factual Describe what you saw without exaggeration – let investigators determine if violations occurred.
