Tips for making an impact

Be specific Include as many details as possible – product names, store address, dates.
Include photos Attach images of the product, packaging, and shelf placement.
Provide location Exact store address helps investigators verify.
Stay respectful & factual Stick to what you observed. Follow up if you don’t hear back.
