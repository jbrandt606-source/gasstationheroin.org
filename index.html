Gas Station Heroin

Kratom products are being sold in smoke shops, vape stores, and gas stations — often in plain sight.

Learn What They Sell
Sold like a convenience product. Marketed like it is harmless.

What parents need to know

This site documents how kratom products appear in ordinary retail spaces, how they are packaged, and why parents should pay attention.

Sold in plain sight

Kratom may be displayed beside vapes, cigars, hookah products, CBD, energy shots, and convenience-store items.

Packaged to look casual

Bright labels, small bottles, gummies, powders, and extract shots can make serious products look routine.

Parents deserve warning

Families should know what is being sold in these stores and how these products are marketed.

Know the sign.
Know the product.

When a storefront advertises kratom beside vape products, parents should understand what that means before their kids do.
