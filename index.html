Gas Station Heroin

The Thesis

Kratom is being sold like a convenience product while parents are left in the dark.

GasStationHeroin.org documents how kratom products appear in smoke shops, vape stores, and gas stations — and why families deserve clear warnings before kids encounter these products first.

What Parents Need to Know

These products are often marketed in ordinary retail spaces, placed beside vapes, CBD, cigars, hookah products, energy shots, and other impulse-buy items.

Sold in Plain Sight

Kratom products may be displayed at counters, on shelves, or in bright packaging that looks routine.

Packaged Casually

Shots, powders, gummies, capsules, and extracts can make serious products appear harmless or familiar.

Parents Need Visibility

The first step is knowing what these stores sell and how these products are being normalized.

Know the Sign.
Know the Product.

When a storefront advertises kratom beside vape products, families should understand what that means before the products become invisible through repetition.

Convenience-store placement does not make a product safe.
