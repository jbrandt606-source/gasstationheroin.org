Gas Station Heroin

Kratom products are being sold in smoke shops, vape stores, and gas stations — often beside candy-colored vapes and energy shots.

This site helps parents recognize the retail environment, the products, and the warning signs.

What Parents Need to Know See How It’s Sold
It is not harmless because it is sold at the counter.

What Parents Need to Know

These products are not tucked away in a pharmacy. They are sold in ordinary retail spaces where kids and families pass by every day.

Sold Like a Casual Product

Kratom may appear as shots, capsules, powders, gummies, or extracts in vape and smoke-shop displays.

Marketed to Look Normal

Bright labels, wellness language, and convenience-store placement can make serious products look routine.

Parents Need Visibility

Knowing what these stores sell is the first step toward recognizing exposure and pushing for accountability.

Look at the Signage.
Look at the Products.

When a storefront advertises kratom beside vapes, cigars, hookah, and CBD, parents should know exactly what that means.
