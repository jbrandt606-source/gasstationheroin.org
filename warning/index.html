WARNING SIGNS
OF TEEN SUBSTANCE USE

Changes in behavior, mood, and habits could be signs your teen is using alcohol or drugs.

Look for these warning signs and start a conversation. Early awareness can save a life.
👪 You are not alone — support is available 24/7.

MOOD CHANGES

  • Irritable, angry, or quick to argue
  • Sudden mood swings
  • Feeling sad, anxious, or “down”
  • Loss of interest in activities they used to enjoy

SCHOOL & ACTIVITY CHANGES

  • Drop in grades or missing school
  • Loss of motivation or not trying
  • Not completing assignments
  • Losing interest in sports, clubs, or hobbies

SECRETIVE BEHAVIOR

  • More private or defensive
  • Lying about plans or whereabouts
  • Hiding things or being vague
  • Excessive phone, text, or social media use

PHYSICAL CHANGES

  • Bloodshot eyes or dilated pupils
  • Sudden weight loss or gain
  • Changes in appetite
  • Poor hygiene or disheveled appearance
  • Frequent colds or unexplained health issues

SLEEP CHANGES

  • Trouble falling or staying asleep
  • Sleeping much more than usual
  • Tired, sluggish, or low energy
  • Odd sleep schedule or staying up all night

SOCIAL CHANGES

  • Withdrawing from family and friends
  • Spending time with a new group
  • Secretive about where they go or who they’re with
  • Changes in online activity or phone use

RISKY OR UNUSUAL BEHAVIOR

  • Taking unnecessary risks
  • Decline in judgment
  • Getting into trouble at school or with the law
  • Unexplained need for money

SIGNS TO LOOK FOR

  • Alcohol, vape, or drug smell
  • Possession of alcohol, drugs, or paraphernalia
  • Missing items or money
  • Evidence of drug use (vapes, edibles, pills, rolling papers, etc.)
💙 YOU ARE NOT ALONE. Early conversations and support can make a big difference.
Reach out for help — you have the power to protect and guide.
