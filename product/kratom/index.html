Look For These Clues

Leaf imagery, green powder, or botanical branding
Words like extract, enhanced, premium, gold, black, ultra, or max strength
Claims about mood, focus, relaxation, energy, pain relief, or sleep
Small liquid shots, tinctures, capsule packs, gummies, candies, or drinks
Products displayed near vape items, smoking products, or checkout counters
Packaging that looks like candy, energy products, wellness drinks, or supplements

Parent Tip

Photograph the front and back of the package before throwing anything away.
Ask where it was purchased and whether it was bought online or in a local store.
Look for serving size, alkaloid content, extract strength, and warning language.
If your child feels sick when stopping, seek medical advice.
