Quick Warning Signs

Packaging and product names change often. These clues can help parents recognize concerning products.

Claims That Sound Too Good

Look for words like euphoric, bliss, relaxation, focus, mood, energy, or extra strength.

Vague Labeling

Products may use terms like botanical specimen, research product, incense, or not for consumption.

Cartoon Packaging

Bright colors, candy themes, fruit flavors, and comic-style artwork may appeal to youth.

Sold Near Vapes

Many products are displayed near vape supplies, behind glass cases, or at checkout counters.

What To Do If You Find A Product

1Stay calm and avoid confrontation.
2Photograph the front and back.
3Save the package and receipt.
4Check the ingredient list.
5Seek medical advice if symptoms appear.
